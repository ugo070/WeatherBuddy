About

WeatherBuddy combines two things that are nearly impossible to avoid: Mother Nature and music. We have provided a way to input your current location, and based on the weather at your location, we will supply you with an awesome playlist from Spotify. From sunshine to snowfall, we have you covered! If you were wishing you were in another city, don't fret! Feel free to input a desired location and get a playlist to compliment the weather there.
